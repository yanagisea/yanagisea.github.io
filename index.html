Biography

I am focusing on Computational Fluid Dynamics(CFD). My research involves aerodynamic analysis and shape optimisation for UAM and flight vehicles, utilising Adjoint methods to improve design efficiency. My background includes aircraft system design and aerodynamic analysis for various flight vehicles, including launch systems.
